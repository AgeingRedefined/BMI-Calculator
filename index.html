About Age-Adjusted BMI

This calculator uses different BMI ranges based on your age:

  • Under 40: Standard BMI ranges (Normal: 18.5-24.9)
  • Ages 40-65: Adjusted ranges (Normal: 20.0-25.0)
  • Over 65: Higher ranges (Normal: 23.0-27.0)

These adjustments reflect the natural changes in body composition and health risks that occur with aging.
